Ratan Tata

Born: December 28, 1937

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded globally, acquiring major companies like Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel.

"I don't believe in taking right decisions. I take decisions and then make them right." - Ratan Tata

He is widely respected for his ethical business values, innovation, and contributions to India's industrial and social sectors. His initiatives in education, healthcare, and entrepreneurship have impacted millions of lives.
